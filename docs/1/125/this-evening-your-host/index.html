This Evening – Your Host

December 2004, Warsaw, by Miss Thoughtscriber

If you ever come near the Krakowskie Przedmieście Street or Warsaw’s Old Town, be sure to have some of the best pierogi in the world at Pierogarnia on Bednarska Street. That’s me after a serving of Russian ones (i.e., with potato, fresh cheese and onion filling).
