Oberbaum Cycling

August 2019, Berlin

One of the exercises at Valérie’s street photograpy workshop was learning to pan – set a long shutter time, follow a movement steadily and try to get a sharp person in front of a dynamic, blurred background. Oberbaum Bridge’s cycling path is a prime place to practice.
